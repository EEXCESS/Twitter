@RecoRobot

A twitter bot

Access cultural data
using Twitter

The @RecoRobot bot has been developed within the EEXCESS project. The vision of EEXCESS is to reveal and interconnect the treasures of European culture, science and education.

Forming a community
by offering data

@RecoRobot is able to autonomously recommend resources based on keywords extracted from twitter status updates. (Keywords are economic sciences related) FOLLOW to get periodical recommendations. MENTION to query the bot.

Open Source
research prototype

The @RecoRobot is implemented in Python3 and released unter the MIT license. The sources can be found in the EEXESS github repository.
